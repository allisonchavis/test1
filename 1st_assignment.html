How to make a PB&J sandwich

  1. Grab ingredients: bread, PB, and Jelly
  2. Grab a spreading knife
  3. Spread PB on one side of one piece of bread
  4. Spread Jelly on one side of the other piece of bread
  5. Smush the PB side to the jelly side
  6. Devour!
